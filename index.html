Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about OmugwoApp. Can't find what you're looking for? We'd love to help.

OmugwoApp is a marketplace platform connecting new mothers in Nigeria with vetted, trained postpartum caregivers. Drawing from the traditional Igbo practice — where family comes to support a new mother — we modernize and formalize that care for today's families.

All caregivers undergo background checks, identity verification, health screenings, and complete our OmugwoApp Academy training programme. We assess cultural competency, newborn care skills, and emotional intelligence before any caregiver is listed.
