Omugwo is a marketplace platform that connects new mothers and families in Nigeria with vetted, trained postpartum caregivers. Drawing from the traditional Igbo practice of "omugwo" — where family comes to support a new mother — we modernize and formalize that care for today's families.

All Omugwo caregivers undergo background checks, identity verification, health screenings, and complete our Omugwo Academy training programme. We assess cultural competency, newborn care skills, and emotional intelligence before any caregiver is listed on our platform.
